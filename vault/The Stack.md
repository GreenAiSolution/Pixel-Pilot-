# The Stack — business brain

Pixel Pilot's full operating base: **41 curated apps, connectors & tools** across 10 business functions. Source: `pixel-pilot/stack.ts` · live page: **/stack** · API: `/api/pixel-pilot/stack`.

Each tool declares its **integration path**:
- **Native** — first-class OAuth we own (lights up *Live* when env creds are set)
- **Via Zapier** — reached through the Catch-Hook bridge (9,000+ apps)
- **Via agent** — through an MCP/agent tool
- **On roadmap** — planned
